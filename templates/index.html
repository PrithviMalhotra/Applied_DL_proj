About Us

Welcome to Prithvi Cafe, a beloved oasis in the heart of Juhu, Mumbai. Nestled next to the iconic Prithvi Theatre, which was founded in 1944 by the legendary actor Prithviraj Kapoor to celebrate and promote Hindi theater, Prithvi Cafe has become more than just a place to eat—it’s a gathering spot for artists, dreamers, and lovers of art.

A Blend of Tradition and Taste
At Prithvi Cafe, we celebrate the harmony of culture and cuisine. Our menu boasts an array of delights, from the rich and velvety Irish coffee and aromatic Suleimani chai to hearty favorites like keema pav and stuffed paranthas. Indulge in comforting plates of pasta, crispy fish-n-chips, and decadent desserts—all at an affordable price, making every visit fulfilling and delicious.

A Haven for the Soul
Experience the serene ambiance that sets Prithvi Cafe apart. With outdoor seating surrounded by lush greenery, it’s an idyllic spot whether you’re seeking solitude, sharing stories with friends, or basking in the vibrant buzz of creative minds.

Rooted in the Heart of Juhu
Conveniently located in Mumbai's lively neighborhood of Juhu, Prithvi Cafe offers more than just great food. Just a short stroll away is the renowned Juhu Beach, where the rhythmic waves and golden sands create a perfect post-meal escape. Dive into art and literature at the Prithvi bookstore, stocked with a thoughtfully curated collection of books on theater, art, and more.

Join us at Prithvi Cafe, where every visit is a blend of history, flavors, and inspiration—a place where stories are shared and memories are made.
